Model Karina Silva is honored today in Luanda

Luanda, 28/04 - Angolan model Karina Silva, second place in the Model of the World 2006 competition, will be honored today in Luanda.

The tribute gala takes place at 10 pm at the São Jorge restaurant on the island of Luanda

According to the Marketing Director of the construction materials company Fermat, institution that supports the event, José Carlos Mourato, the gala is promoted by Black Out Production, Frente Nova and São Jorge restaurant.

The 2006 edition of the Models of the World was held in March in the Tanzanian city Dar es Salaam and had the participation of more than 100 candidates

The annual event aims to promote the image and socio-cultural values of the participating countries, as well as the discovery of new talents. This is the first edition of the Angolan model.

Born in Benguela, the 1.80 m tall mannequin began her career at the age of 14. And she is considered the most international Angolan model.

At national level, she has participated in all fashion events, from Moda Luanda, Angola Fashion Week and other shows in the capital and some provinces, having been distinguished three times with the award for best female model.

Internationally walked the catwalk in several countries, including France and Portugal.
