Luanda has a new materials store

Luanda, 06/10 - A construction materials store will be inaugurated this evening in the Cuca neighborhood in Luanda, by the company Fermat, which belongs to the Importrading Group.

When providing information to ANGOP, today in Luanda, the Marketing Director, José Carlos Mourato, referred that the creation of the enterprise is part of the celebration of the 12th anniversary of the firm and the expansion of its activities.

Fermat was founded on October 10, 1994, with a share capital of USD 800,000.00 employing 32 employees

The Company already has two stores in operation in Luanda and one in the City of Lubango, province of Huíla
